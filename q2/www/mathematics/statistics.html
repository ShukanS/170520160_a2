Florence Nightingale

Professor Biography


“The Lady with the Lamp”, Florence Nightingale, was an influential social reformer and statistician. However, she is most famous for being the founder of modern nursing, overseeing and training nurses during the Crimean War, aiding wounded soldiers at night. Nightingale is also known for being a pioneer in the field of visual stats, and statistical graphics.
