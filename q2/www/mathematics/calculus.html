Sir Isaac Newton

Professor Biography


Sir Isaac Newton is a name known to common households around the world. Newton is known for developing the laws of motion and gravitation, studied by students around the world. Newton also built the first reflecting telescope through his research on colour and light. The “natural philosopher” is a monumental figure in the field of mathematics and science as a whole.
