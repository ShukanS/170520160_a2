Ada Lovelace

Professor Biography


Ada Lovelace was a pivotal figure in the early history of computers. Known for her mathematics and computing, Professor Lovelace published the first algorithm for a machine. She was one of the first computer programmers, and was a pioneer of the computer programming field.
